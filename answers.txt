Pros:
Allows for constant communication with customers
Provides value to customer during development
Cons:
Requires constant coordination and meetings
Allows for scope creep

Scrum master:
Facilitates sprints and meetings 
Product Owner: 
handles release and stakeholder management. sets team direction.

Sprint Planning meeting: 1) Move stories from backlog into sprint. 2) Assign stories to team members
Daily Scrum/Standup: 1) What happened on the given day regarding the sprint. 2) Discuss and identify blockers.
Sprint retrospective: 1)Discuss completed work 2) move incomplete work to backlog.
